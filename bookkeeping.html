Bookkeeping and accounting

Cleaner books, clearer reports, and better financial visibility.

Business Tax Prime supports small businesses with practical bookkeeping and accounting organization, especially through collaborative QuickBooks Online workflows.

QuickBooks Online support

Client-owned records with secure accountant access

Clients maintain ownership and visibility of their QuickBooks Online account while Business Tax Prime assists through secure accountant access. This keeps financial information accessible to the client while allowing the BTP team to support reconciliations, reporting, categorization, and tax preparation.

The result is a collaborative accounting process that is organized, transparent, and easier to review throughout the year.
