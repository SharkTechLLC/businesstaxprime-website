Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Business Tax Prime, LLC handles information through its website and business services.

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Business Tax Prime, LLC ("Business Tax Prime," "we," "us," or "our") respects privacy and aims to collect only the information needed to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, operate our website, and maintain our business records.

Information we may collect

When you contact us, request information, schedule a consultation, submit a form, or work with us as a client, we may collect information you voluntarily provide. This may include your name, email address, phone number, business information, appointment details, service inquiries, messages, and records needed to provide accounting, bookkeeping, tax, payroll, insurance referral, business, or technology-related services.

How information may be used

We may use information to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, provide requested services, maintain business records, communicate with clients or prospective clients, comply with legal and professional obligations, and improve our website or service operations.

Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, professional software platforms, payment processors, scheduling providers, communication tools, accounting or tax platforms, insurance referral providers, legal or compliance advisors, or other third parties when needed to operate our business, provide requested services, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Third-party services and links

Our website and communications may link to third-party websites, platforms, tools, insurance resources, scheduling services, tax platforms, or payment services. Those third parties operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Data security and confidentiality

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information we handle. No website, email, or electronic storage method is completely secure. Sensitive documents should be exchanged only through approved secure methods.

Children's privacy

Business Tax Prime services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

You may contact us to request updates or corrections to information you have provided to Business Tax Prime, subject to legal, tax, accounting, recordkeeping, and professional obligations that may require us to retain certain information.

Mobile apps privacy

Privacy practices for mobile apps published by Business Tax Prime, LLC are described separately in the Mobile Apps Privacy Policy.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as our services, technology, or legal requirements change. The effective date above reflects the latest version.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact Business Tax Prime, LLC at ContactUs@BusinessTaxPrime.com.
